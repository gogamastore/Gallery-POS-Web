# **App Name**: NexusWholesale

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Management: Admins can add, edit, view, and delete wholesale products, managing inventory levels, pricing, and product details.
- Bulk Order Processing: Monitor and update the status of wholesale orders from creation to fulfillment, including mass actions and detailed order views.
- Wholesale Metrics Dashboard: Visualize key wholesale sales metrics, inventory performance, and order summaries through interactive cards and data tables.
- AI Sales & Market Insights Tool: Utilize an AI-powered tool to generate actionable insights and recommendations on sales trends, product demand, and potential market opportunities based on historical data.
- Admin Control Panel: Manage overall store status (Open/Closed), access global search functionality for products and orders, and manage admin profiles.
- Reseller Account Management: View and manage registered reseller accounts, including contact details and order history specific to each reseller.
- Sales Report Generation: Generate comprehensive sales reports for specific periods or product categories to aid business analysis and strategy.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual design takes cues from professionalism, data clarity, and business efficiency. A light color scheme ensures optimal readability. The primary color is a composed yet deep blue (#2259C7), signifying reliability and focus on insights. The background color is a heavily desaturated light blue (#ECF0F7), providing a clean canvas. An accent color of bright teal (#3DBFDA) provides a vibrant contrast for interactive elements and highlights, suggesting innovation and clarity.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif. Its objective and neutral aesthetic provides excellent readability for diverse content types on a data-heavy dashboard.
- Use a consistent set of minimalist, outline-style icons that clearly represent their respective functions, aligning with the professional and data-focused nature of the dashboard. Icons should be easily discernible at various sizes.
- Implement a clear three-column dashboard layout: a fixed left sidebar for navigation, a top header for essential actions and global search, and the main content area structured with a flexible grid for metric cards and data tables. Prioritize intuitive information hierarchy and efficient use of space.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations for UI feedback, such as hover states, modal transitions, and data loading. Animations should enhance user experience without causing distraction or delays.
